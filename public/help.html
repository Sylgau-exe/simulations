Getting Started

ProjectManagerTool is a marketplace of curated project management tools designed for professionals. We bring together the best PM tools in one place, making it easy to find, compare, and access the tools you need to manage projects effectively. Whether you're looking for Gantt charts, Kanban boards, time trackers, or collaboration tools, we've got you covered.
Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign In" button in the top navigation, then select "Create Account." Enter your email address and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can start exploring and activating tools right away.
Yes! We offer a free plan that gives you access to 3 tools. It's perfect for trying out the platform and seeing if it fits your workflow. When you're ready for more, you can upgrade to one of our paid plans for unlimited access and additional features.
Browse our tools catalog and click on any tool you're interested in. You'll see a detailed description, features, and user ratings. Click "Activate Tool" to add it to your dashboard. Depending on your plan, you may have a limit on how many tools you can activate at once.

Plans & Billing

We offer four plans to fit different needs:

Free: Access to 3 tools, perfect for trying us out
Starter ($9/mo): Access to 10 tools
Professional ($19/mo): Access to 25 tools + priority support
Unlimited ($49/mo): Unlimited tools + all features + dedicated support
Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through our secure payment processor, Stripe. All transactions are encrypted and your payment information is never stored on our servers.
You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Click on "Billing," then "Cancel Subscription." You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but you won't be charged again after cancellation.
