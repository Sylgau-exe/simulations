Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BizSimHub

What is BizSimHub?

BizSimHub is a business simulation platform that helps professionals and students master business skills through immersive, hands-on simulations. Our flagship product, Project Apex, offers PMP-aligned project management training.

Is it suitable for PMP prep?

Absolutely! Our simulations are designed with PMBOK-aligned concepts, making them ideal for PMP certification preparation. You'll practice real-world scenarios involving scope, schedule, budget, and team management.

How long are the simulations?

Each simulation session typically takes 30-60 minutes. However, you can save progress and return anytime. Complete scenarios can be replayed to try different strategies.

Can I use it for my team?

Yes! Our Enterprise plan includes team management features, analytics dashboards, and LMS integration. Contact us for volume pricing and custom scenarios.

What industries are covered?

Project Apex covers Tech Startup, Live Entertainment, Construction, and R&D Innovation. Each industry presents unique challenges and learning opportunities.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a free tier that includes access to Project Apex with one industry scenario. Professional plans include a 7-day free trial with full access.
